Introduction

The following visualizations represent various demographic statistics within Community Areas in the city of Chicago, Illinois, USA. The map and chart are coordinated so that moving your mouse over one will indicate where on the other visual the sample area is represented. A tooltip also appears displaying the selected statistic for the sample area.

Data Sources

All datasets were obtained from resources published by ESRI and the City of Chicago. The datasets published by ESRI were derived from the U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey (ACS) 2014-2018 5-year estimates. Click here for a full listing of data sources and attribute definitions.
